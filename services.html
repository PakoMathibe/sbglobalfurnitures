Professional Services

These services aim to provide a seamless and comprehensive experience for our customers, from the initial design phase to the final delivery and beyond. We strive to exceed customer expectations and ensure their satisfaction at every step of their furniture journey.

Furniture Manufacturing

We have a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility equipped with advanced machinery and skilled artisans who create high-quality furniture. Our manufacturing process follows stringent quality control measures to ensure that each piece meets our standards of excellence.

Custom Design

We understand that every customer has unique preferences and requirements. Our experienced design team can work closely with clients to create custom furniture pieces tailored to their specific needs, whether it's a unique sofa design, a customized dining table, or bespoke bedroom furniture.

Retail and Showrooms

SB Global Furniture operates retail outlets and showrooms where customers can explore and purchase our wide range of furniture. These physical locations provide an immersive shopping experience, allowing customers to see and feel the quality of our products firsthand.

Project and Contract

We collaborate with architects, interior designers, and commercial clients to fulfill large-scale projects and contract furnishing needs. Whether it's furnishing a hotel, office building, restaurant, or any other commercial space, our team can provide comprehensive solutions tailored to the project's requirements.

Delivery and Installation

We offer reliable delivery services to ensure that our furniture reaches customers safely and on time. Additionally, our professional installation team can assemble and set up furniture at the customer's location, providing a hassle-free experience.
